Dynamic AMOLED 2X

Unbelievably bright. Incredibly vivid. Surprisingly smooth — this display shines with vibrant color and amazing clarity, delivering an unparalleled viewing experience. And that's even in bright sunlight.

*The Dynamic AMOLED 2X display on Galaxy S21 5G and S21+ 5G received a certification from VDE Germany for 100 percent Mobile Color Volume in the DCI-P3 color range, which means your images aren’t washed out and you’ll get unbelievably vivid colors regardless of differing levels of brightness.
